About Me

Beginning from 2013 I'm a photographer and work with reportage shootings, portrait, fashion and street photos. I've made more than 110 portrait photo shoots. I also do videography and video editing.

I am a designer of 3 clothes collections - "FAB" in 2019 for an international project for a special occasion - 10th Anniversary of the Faberge Museum in Baden-Baden, "DEEP" in 2020 as a project dedicated for people with congenital skin changes and "AQUARIUM" in 2022 inspired by Josip Broz Tito and his vacation villa "Galeb".

I've been a costume designer for 6 plays in different cities of Russia such as St. Petersburg, Sergiev Posad, Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk and Tver.
